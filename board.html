Maryan Damey

Director

Maryan Damey is a dynamic and accomplished 23-year-old Kenyan Advocate and English Solicitor. She is a rising star in the legal field, known for her exceptional skills and dedication. Maryan has already made a significant impact in her career, specializing in commercial law, with a focus on financial, corporate, and taxation matters. She has a strong background in project finance, capital markets, banking, and foreign investments. Maryan holds an LLB from the University of Nairobi and was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2019.

Sharon Gathoni

Board Member

As a young and dynamic board member of DameyBank at the age of 25, Sharon is a visionary leader with a passion for innovation and excellence. She's one of the co-founder of DameyBank and currently serves as its Chairperson, demonstrating her strategic thinking and business acumen. Sharon's contributions to the banking industry have been recognized with prestigious awards, including the Moran of the Order of the Burning Spear in December 2002. In addition to her role at DameyBank, Sharon is actively involved in various philanthropic endeavors, reflecting her commitment to making a positive impact in the community.
