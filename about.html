DameyBank Group Plc

DameyBank, a subsidiary of Damey Corporation, is a leading financial institution committed to providing innovative banking solutions since its establishment in 2024. With a rich history of over three decades, DameyBank has grown to become a trusted name in the banking industry, known for its reliability, integrity, and customer-centric approach.

DameyBank is dedicated to fostering financial growth and prosperity for individuals, businesses, and communities across Kenya. As a fully licensed bank regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya, DameyBank offers a wide range of banking services, including savings and current accounts, loans, investments, and digital banking solutions.

At DameyBank, we believe in the power of partnership and collaboration. We have established strategic alliances with leading financial institutions in the region, enabling us to provide our customers with access to a comprehensive suite of financial products and services.

Our commitment to excellence and innovation has earned us numerous accolades and recognition in the banking sector. We are proud to be a part of the vibrant Kenyan economy, contributing to its growth and development.
